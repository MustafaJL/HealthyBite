About us

Fitabelle is a trusted authority in nutritional counseling, dedicated to guiding individuals toward their health and wellness goals. Led by an expert dietitian with a master's degree in clinical nutrition from Beirut Arab University, Fitabelle offers personalized, evidence-based advice for managing health conditions and adopting a healthy lifestyle. Our approach moves beyond restrictive diets, focusing instead on sustainable and enjoyable eating habits that align with each person's unique needs and preferences. We view nutrition as a holistic practice that not only fuels the body but also nourishes the mind and soul. Committed to providing the knowledge and tools necessary for informed nutritional decisions, Fitabelle has successfully helped many clients achieve long-term positive changes and enhanced overall well-being.
